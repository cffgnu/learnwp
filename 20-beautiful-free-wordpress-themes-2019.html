As soon as you install WordPress , the next job is probably installing a WordPress theme . Themes and plugins are the two things that make WordPress flexible and powerful. 

Talking about themes, you have 2 choices: free and paid themes. With the premium theme , you will get many things such as complete documentation, powerful features, and support when you get stuck. Of course, you will have to pay a fee in return. 

If you’re just starting a website and don’t have much budget, a free theme is the way to go. That said, it doesn’t mean that all free themes are “sloppy”. 

Many theme providers have “generously” launched many free versions of themes with limited features compared to the paid version. But you still have a theme with a beautiful interface, SEO optimized and fast loading. ​

You will notice this in today’s collection of beautiful free WordPress themes.

Are you curious about them?

Let’s just start exploring.

Theme Bento

Although Bento has a reputation as a free theme, it is equipped with a range of features that you only find in paid themes. 

Theme is designed with good quality, many powerful features. The theme is very easy to use and customize. 

Feature:

  • There is a drag and drop content builder that makes it easy to design professional layouts without knowing coding
  • Compatible with Woocomerce so you can use the theme as a very good sales website. 
  • Built-in 500+ Google Fonts so you can easily choose the font you want. 
  • There is a one-page layout so you can design a website to introduce your company or products or services

5 best places to buy WordPress themes for newbies

Tutorials 0 lượt xem June 30, 2022
30
Jun

Besides the number of free WordPress themes on WordPress.org, there will certainly be times when you will intend to buy a paid theme.

Why?

Of course, regular paid stuff is better than free stuff. This is not something we discuss.

With a paid theme you get a lot of benefits compared to a free theme

  • Paid themes usually have a nicer interface and more features
  • You can get support whenever you have difficulty
  • Documentation and demo data are always complete
  • Code is often optimized for SEO and speed

But currently on the WordPress theme market, the number of paid theme providers is quite large. This number is enough to overwhelm you with the question of which supplier to choose. Each provider has its own advantages and disadvantages.

Understanding this problem, I want to share some reputable WordPress theme providers. The criteria I chose were based on price, theme quality, and beginner-friendly.

Now let’s explore together the websites that sell the best WordPress themes.

1. Theme Junkies​

If you are a WordPress lover and have little experience with it, then the name Theme Junkie is no stranger. I rate this as a cheap and good quality theme provider.

The theme here has a characteristic simple design. The theme option itself is equally simple. You don’t get dizzy by having too many complicated options. So the theme here is extremely suitable for those who are just starting to get acquainted with WordPress.

You don’t spend too much time learning how to use the theme. Plus you can easily install demo data with just one click without having to import xml. 

Read more :

How to build a professional blog using Theme Junkie’s Beginner theme

Theme junkie discount code

The downside here is that the theme is not updated regularly and the speed of new themes is also quite slow. The theme has few options which means you can’t do too much customization.

Up to now, Theme Junkie has over 40 paid themes. Support via forum and response time is also quite fast.

Regarding the price, Theme Junkie offers 3 options as below:

  • Single Theme: buy a theme for $39
  • All Themes Package: buy a full set of themes for $49. You will get support and updates within a year
  • Lifetime package: one-time payment for $99. You will get lifetime updates and support. ​

2. MyThemeShop

MyThemeShop is one of the theme providers with an extremely large number of customers.

Currently, this provider has over 100 themes and over 23 plugins with a customer base of more than 300,000 customers. 

The theme here is famous for its speed and SEO optimization. In general, the quality of the theme here is very good.

Besides, MyThemeShop is very diligent in plugin development. The plugins here are quite quality and useful. 

MyThemeShop also offers many free themes and plugins to give you a chance to evaluate the theme quality here. From there you have a basis to decide to spend money to buy a theme or plugin.

The themes here are suitable for all types of websites: blog, business, ecommerce and magazine. Personally, I think MyThemeShop is very strong in the theme for personal blogs. 

The themes are very easy to use. The theme options section is relatively rich and easy to use.

Especially the import demo is extremely simple and convenient. You can import or even delete demo data after import with just one click. 

I am very impressed with the quality of themes and plugins of MyThemeShop. It gives me the inspiration to regularly create articles about the company’s products. Check out my series of MyThemeShop articles

3. Themify

The themes here have a very nice design and fully meet your needs from blogging to e-commerce. Control Panel with many options to help you freely customize the theme.

The themes here have a sleek and modern design. Themify offers a variety of themes to meet your needs from blogging to e-commerce.

Personally, I find the theme here extremely suitable for those of you who want to have a website with a landing page that impresses readers. Landing page is currently the trend of current business website. Because well designed landing pages for very high conversions. 

The strong point of the themes here is that they are all equipped with the “ Drag & Drop Website Builder ” feature. This themify builder is very lightweight compared to Visual Composer . You can design pages in both backend and frontend. Themify also bundles the page builder into its own plugin for customers who don’t use its themes.​

You can try out a version of Themify Builder lite on WordPress.org.​ The downside of Themify builder is the small number of components. Important components are packaged as add-ons and sold separately.

4. Elegant Theme

​Elegant Themes is also a theme provider with a large number of customers. The theme here is famous for its beautiful delicate design. 

Read more : Evaluate Elegant Themes whether the theme here is right for you

The highlight of Elegant Themes is the Divi Builder that makes it easy to design websites without knowing coding. Divi Builder is available in two main themes, Divi and Extra.

In addition, Elegant Themes also bundles Divi Builder as a plugin so you can use this builder for any other theme.

In my opinion, if you are a newbie, you need to take some time to learn how to use the theme. Especially Divi Builder requires you to read the documentation to know how to use it.

In general, the theme here is suitable for those who want to design an interface themselves.

Read more : Instructions for setting up a simple blog using Theme Extra

The support via the forum is not very fast. Probably due to the large number of customers. Although there are many staff members participating in support, but if I open a few tickets, it will take 1 to a few days to see a reply. 

Regarding the price, Elegant Themes does not sell themes or plugins individually. This is also a pity because many of you love some of its plugins but don’t want both the theme and the plugin.

5. ThemeForest

ThemeForest is actually the largest WordPress theme marketplace in the world. The number of themes is too much. Theme prices also fluctuate for each theme. 

Besides, you can buy the plugin through another marketplace called Codecanyon also under Evanto Market. 

In general, the themes and plugins sold here have gone through the market’s quality assessment. So the main problem is that you have to choose the right theme or plugin for you. 

First you need to see the demo to get an overview. Then you refer to the number of sales and the name of the seller. 

Many of these themes tend to pack a lot of functionality and integrate a lot of paid plugins. 

Many functions will give you a lot of customization power but at the same time can affect the speed of the website. 

So when using it, you need to turn off the functions you don’t need.
